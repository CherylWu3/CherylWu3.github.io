About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Economics at Yale University. My research focuses on the intersection of growth, economic history, and political economy, with particular emphasis on technology adoption and innovation.

Outside of my academic work, I enjoy photography, playing the piano, and baking.

Research interests: growth, economic history, AI, political economy, innovation, technology adoption
